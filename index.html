Welcome to BOYNEXTDOOR

Who's there? the BOYNEXTDOOR!

Our Songs Join Fandom

The Onedoorfull Place

Boynextdoor is a South Korean boy group that debuted in 2023 under the label KOZ Entertainment. The group is known for its fresh sound and energetic performances, blending various musical styles like pop, R&B, and hip-hop. With a diverse lineup of talented members, Boynextdoor showcases strong vocals, impressive dance skills, and engaging stage presence.

  • Fun and Professional
  • Diverse and Quality Songs
  • King of Stage Presence Gen5

Their music often features themes of youth, love, and self-discovery, resonating with fans both in South Korea and internationally. The group quickly gained popularity for their catchy tracks, visually appealing concepts, and relatable lyrics. With a growing fanbase, Boynextdoor continues to make a mark in the K-pop industry, pushing boundaries and setting trends.
